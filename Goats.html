    • Goats

    Overview

    Goats are farm animals and are one of the most useful animals for industrial purposes as they can provide wool, milk and meat. They need to have a large pasture to graze in. It is best to keep them in large herds.

    Diet and Habitat

    They are herbivores and need to eat grass and vegetables everyday.

    Threats & Diseases

    To take good care you should brush to check for lice or rashes and clip their hooves to prevent joint problems or footrot. Eye infections can be caused by hairloss, crustiness or swelling in the eye. To treat this it is best to use veterinarian prescribed eye-drops. They can also suffer from pneumonia from dust particles, hot wheather or a weak immune system. To prevent pneumonia yu should clean the living space regularly to prevent dust build up.

    Fun Facts:

    Their pupils are rectangular to give them a wider view, but they can't look up or down without moving their head.
