    Overview

    Rabbits are a type of small farm animals, they are one of the farm animals that can also be kept as pets. There are nearly 350 million rabbits worldwide. It is better to have more than one rabbit or another pet besides the rabbit to keep it company.

    Diet and Habitat

    They are herbivores and can live upto 12 years if taken care of properly. To have a healthy rabbit you must feed it at least 3 times a day, it should also have hay to chew on otherwise its teeth may grow too much and will be painful for the rabbit. Wild rabbits have a rich diet and can look for their own food as per their needs. Wild rabbits mostly live in burrows to hide from predetors such as hawks, eagles, foxes etc. For living conditions for a domestic rabbit; there should be an open space for jumping and being active and a clean water should be provided everyday.

    Threats & Diseases

    Since, rabbits are a semi-domesticated animal they have their own cleaning habits like cats and they can develop hairballs. To prevenet this we should feed it fiborous foods.
